# Enterprise (Dublin–Belfast) service

## Station pairing
- Dublin: **Dublin Connolly**
- Belfast: **Belfast Grand Central**

## Onboard Catering
From the trolley service snacks to full meals from the dining carriage, you'll have fabulous food and refreshments for your journey. We have vegan and gluten free options on board.

## Travelling beyond the station
Your Enterprise ticket will take you there! A valid Enterprise rail ticket lets you take the train between Grand Central Station and Titanic Quarter Station! Your ticket to Dublin will get you on the red line Luas between Connolly and Heuston too, so you can see more when you travel by rail.
