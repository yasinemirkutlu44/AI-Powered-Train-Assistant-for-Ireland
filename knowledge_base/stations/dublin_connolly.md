# Dublin Connolly (Dublin)

Connolly is the default Dublin station for the **Enterprise** cross‑border service to Belfast and a major hub for DART/commuter services and some InterCity routes.

## Location & contact
- **Address:** Amiens Street, Dublin 1, D01 V6V6 (from station page).
- Station phone numbers are listed on the station page.

## Hours (published on the station page)
- **Booking office:** Mon–Sat 07:00–19:00; Sun closed
- **Enclosed waiting room:** 06:30–21:00 daily

## Facilities
- Toilets available; station page references a **Changing Places** toilet and instructs customers to ask at the information desk for access.
- ATM in the waiting area (per station page).
- Free Wi‑Fi listed; refreshments outlets listed (subject to change).

## Parking / bikes
- Car parking privately operated (directions on station page).
- Sheltered bike parking and limited rentable bike lockers mentioned on the station page.

## When to recommend Connolly
- Default Dublin departure/arrival station for **Belfast (Grand Central)** via Enterprise.
