# Galway (Ceannt)

Ceannt Station is Galway’s central station near Eyre Square.

## Travel alerts (station page)
- The station page may show temporary notices (e.g., booking office closure or parking restrictions). For near-term travel, check the station page.

## Location & contact
- **Address:** Céannt Station, Galway, H91 T9CE (from station page).
- Local booking office/ticket enquiries phone and lost-property email are listed on the station page.

## Hours (published on station page)
- **Staffing hours:** Mon–Fri 06:00–23:30
- **Booking office:** Mon–Fri 06:00–13:45 (excluding bank holidays); station page may also show temporary closures.

## Facilities
- Toilets, shelters, enclosed waiting room, free Wi‑Fi listed.
- Ticket vending machine available.
- **Smart card enabled:** listed as **No** on station page.
- Refreshments and a water refill unit are listed.

## Routes serviced (examples on station page)
- Dublin Heuston ↔ Galway
- Galway ↔ Limerick
- Connection corridors via Limerick Junction

## Parking & access
- Car parking is privately operated; station page provides directions due to one-way traffic around Eyre Square.
