# Cork (Kent)

Kent Station is Cork’s principal rail station for InterCity services to Dublin and Cork commuter lines.

## Hours (published on station page)
- **Staffing hours:** Mon–Sun 05:00–00:01
- **Booking office:** Mon–Fri 06:40–18:20 (excluding bank holidays); Sat 06:40–15:00; Sun closed

## Facilities
- Toilets, shelters, enclosed waiting room, CCTV and free Wi‑Fi are listed on the station page.
- Ticket vending machines available; smart card enabled.

## Routes serviced (examples on station page)
- Dublin Heuston ↔ Cork (directs and intermediate)
- Mallow ↔ Cobh/Midleton
- Waterford ↔ Clonmel ↔ Limerick Junction (connections)

## Parking & onward transport (from station page)
- APCOA car park: **385 spaces**; disabled bays listed; opening hours and EV charging bays listed.
- Bike facilities: sheltered bike parking; rentable bike lockers (**20 spaces**) noted; bike-share is also referenced.
- Taxi rank outside; WAV registry link provided.

## Assistant hint
- For “Cork ↔ Dublin” queries, map to **Cork (Kent)** ↔ **Dublin Heuston**.
