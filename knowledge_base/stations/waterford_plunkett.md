# Waterford (Plunkett)

Plunkett Station is Waterford’s main station for Dublin–Waterford services.

## Hours (published on station page)
- **Station opening hours:** Mon–Sat 05:30–23:00; Sun 08:30–21:30; Bank Holidays 06:30–21:30
- **Booking office:** Mon–Sat 06:15–13:15; Sun closed

## Facilities
- Toilets, shelters, enclosed waiting room, free Wi‑Fi listed on station page.
- Ticket vending machines available.
- **Smart card enabled:** listed as **No** on station page.

## Parking notice (station page)
- Station page includes a customer notice that demand can be extremely high and the car park may be full early on certain weekdays. Suggest arriving early or using alternatives.

## Parking & onward transport (from station page)
- APCOA car park: **145 spaces**, disabled bays listed; EV charging bays listed; 24-hour operation; charges link.
- Taxi search links and WAV registry link provided.

## Routes serviced (examples on station page)
- Dublin Heuston ↔ Waterford
- Connection corridor via Limerick Junction
