# Belfast (Grand Central)

Belfast Grand Central is Translink’s Belfast transport hub and a terminus for cross‑border rail services.

## Access hours (Translink station page)
- Open 04:30–00:00 for all passengers.
- Between 00:00 and 04:30, Translink describes “night gate” access for passengers with a valid ticket or buying a ticket for a near-term departure.

## Customer support hours (Translink station page)
- Mon–Sat 06:30–20:00
- Sun 07:30–18:00
