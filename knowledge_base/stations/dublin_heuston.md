# Dublin Heuston (Dublin)

Heuston is Dublin’s main **InterCity** station for routes to the south, southwest and west (Cork, Limerick, Galway, Waterford, and others).

## Location & contact
- **Address:** St. Johns Road West, Dublin 8 (see station page map).
- Irish Rail station pages refer general customer-care queries to the Transport for Ireland (TFI) customer contact centre (hours listed on the station page).

## Hours (published on the station page)
- **Station staffing hours:** Mon–Fri 05:00–00:00; Sat 05:30–00:00; Sun 07:00–22:30
- **Booking office:** Mon–Sat 06:30–19:00 and 19:30–21:00; Sun & bank holidays closed

## Tickets & facilities
- Ticket vending machines available; smart card enabled (per station page).
- Free station Wi‑Fi (per station page).
- Toilets and passenger shelters listed; “left luggage” guidance on station page.
- Multiple refreshments outlets and a water refill unit listed (subject to change).
- Phone charging kiosk available on the concourse.

## Common routing guidance
- Default **Dublin → Cork/Galway/Limerick/Waterford** departures to **Heuston** unless user specifies another station.
- For **Belfast/Enterprise**, default to **Dublin Connolly**.
