# Limerick (Colbert)

Colbert Station serves Limerick city and connects with Dublin and Galway corridors.

## Hours (published on station page)
- **Station opening hours:** Mon–Sat 05:00–23:30; Sun 06:30–22:00
- **Booking office:** Mon–Fri 06:00–18:00; Sat 06:00–13:00; Sun closed

## Facilities
- Toilets and a **Changing Places** toilet are listed (ask staff for access).
- Free Wi‑Fi listed.
- Ticket vending machines available.
- **Smart card enabled:** listed as **No** on station page.
- Refreshments: Starbucks listed (subject to change).

## Parking & transport links (from station page)
- APCOA car park: **300 spaces**, disabled bays listed; 24-hour operation; charges link.
- Taxi rank details and WAV register link provided.

## Routes serviced (examples on station page)
- Dublin Heuston ↔ Limerick (two corridors listed)
- Galway ↔ Limerick
- Connection corridor via Limerick Junction
